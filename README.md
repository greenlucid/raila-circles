Borrow, lend, or relay loans using the Circles' directional graph of trust.

### Usage

[Open the app](https://web-ashy-six-87.vercel.app/).

Follow the instructions to import your Metri/Gnosis App wallet.

Enable the Raila Module.

Now you can borrow from people that have USDC.e and trust you, or from people connected via trust to those and whose paths would be compatible.
